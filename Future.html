Humans are not finished

The strongest future is cooperation: people setting goals and AI helping with tasks.

Human skills in an AI world

AI can generate answers, images and computer code, but it does not automatically understand human values. People are still needed to ask meaningful questions, review results, identify mistakes and make ethical decisions. The future of work is likely to reward people who can combine technical understanding with communication, creativity and problem-solving.

Important idea: AI literacy means knowing how to use AI, how to question it, and when not to trust it.
